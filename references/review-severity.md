# Review Severity Guide

- **Critical** — active exploit, severe data loss, broad outage, or irreversible corruption likely.
- **High** — requirement failure, authorization bypass, substantial regression, or significant operational risk.
- **Medium** — important edge case, maintainability defect likely to cause future errors, or incomplete recovery behavior.
- **Low** — limited-impact defect or improvement with clear value; not a stylistic preference.

Severity describes impact and likelihood, not reviewer confidence. Report confidence separately.
